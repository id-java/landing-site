Salsa a la Parth

1 can black beans 1 lime 1 avocado 1/4 red onion, diced 1/2 cup corn 1 tomato 1 green jalapeno, sliced and diced 1/4 tsp cumin powder cilantro pinch of pepper salt to taste 

                    How to make it edible:

1. Rinse and drain the black beans, and add them to a pot
2. Add diced onions, jalapenos, tomato, and corn to the pot
3. Chop your avocado into 1/8" x 1/8" cubes and add to the mix
4. Add the salt, cumin, lime, and pepper
5. Mix CAREFULLY. Make sure you're not mashing the avocado and black beans into a paste.
6. Consume more than you had promised yourself because this mix is somehow actually really good(??)


I best enjoy this with cup style chips!
